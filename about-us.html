Our History

St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management (SAITM) was established in 2005 with a vision to provide quality technical education. Affiliated to Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak, and approved by AICTE, SAITM has grown into a premier institution offering:

  • B.Tech in Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, and Electronics Engineering
  • MBA programs with various specializations
  • BCA and BBA programs
  • Diploma courses in Engineering

Our sprawling 10-acre campus in Gurugram features state-of-the-art infrastructure including:

  • Modern classrooms with smart boards
  • Advanced computer and engineering labs
  • Well-stocked library with digital resources
  • Sports facilities and hostel accommodation
