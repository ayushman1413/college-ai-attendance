SAITM Sports and Recreation

At SAITM, we believe in the holistic development of our students. Our state-of-the-art sports facilities provide students with opportunities to excel in various sports and recreational activities, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and leadership skills.

Our Sports Facilities

  • Cricket ground
  • Football field
  • Basketball court
  • Volleyball court
  • Badminton court
  • Table tennis
  • Chess and carrom
  • Athletics track
  • Gymnasium
  • Yoga and meditation area

Sports Events and Competitions

SAITM organizes various sports events and competitions throughout the academic year to encourage student participation and foster a spirit of healthy competition:

  • Annual Sports Meet
  • Inter-departmental tournaments
  • Inter-college competitions
  • Friendly matches with other institutions
  • Sports workshops and training camps

Sports Achievements

Our students have represented SAITM in various district, state, and national level competitions, bringing laurels to the institution. Some notable achievements include:

  • Winners of Inter-College Cricket Tournament 2023
  • Runners-up in State Level Basketball Championship 2022
  • Gold Medal in District Level Athletics Meet 2023
  • Champions of MDU Volleyball Tournament 2022
  • Individual achievements in various sports at state and national levels

Sports Equipment

SAITM provides high-quality sports equipment for various sports and games. Students can borrow equipment from the sports department during designated hours.

Outdoor Sports
  • Cricket kits
  • Footballs
  • Volleyballs
  • Basketballs
  • Athletics equipment
Indoor Sports
  • Table tennis sets
  • Badminton rackets and shuttlecocks
  • Chess boards
  • Carrom boards
  • Yoga mats

Sports Scholarships

SAITM offers sports scholarships to students who excel in sports at state, national, or international levels. The scholarship includes:

  • Tuition fee waiver (partial or full)
  • Free hostel accommodation
  • Special diet allowance
  • Sports kit and equipment
  • Professional coaching
