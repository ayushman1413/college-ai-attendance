SAITM Canteen Services

The SAITM canteen provides nutritious and delicious meals to students and staff in a clean and hygienic environment. Our canteen is designed to cater to diverse tastes and dietary requirements, ensuring that everyone finds something to enjoy.

Canteen Features

  • Spacious seating arrangement
  • Hygienic food preparation
  • Variety of food options
  • Affordable prices
  • Special diet options available
  • Fresh and quality ingredients
  • Regular health and safety inspections
  • Digital payment options

Food Safety and Hygiene

At SAITM, we prioritize food safety and hygiene. Our canteen follows strict protocols to ensure that all food is prepared and served in a clean environment:

  • Regular health check-ups for canteen staff
  • Daily cleaning and sanitization of the kitchen and dining area
  • Use of fresh and quality ingredients
  • Proper waste management system
  • Regular pest control measures
  • Compliance with food safety regulations
