SAITM Bus Transportation Service

SAITM provides comprehensive bus transportation services to ensure that students and staff can commute safely and conveniently to and from the campus. Our fleet of modern buses covers various routes across Gurugram and neighboring areas.

Key Features:

  • Modern fleet of well-maintained buses
  • Experienced and professional drivers
  • GPS tracking for real-time location monitoring
  • Multiple routes covering major residential areas
  • Flexible pickup and drop-off points
  • Affordable transportation fees
  • Air-conditioned buses for comfortable travel
  • Regular maintenance and safety checks

Bus Schedule

Our buses operate on a fixed schedule to ensure punctuality:

  • Morning Pickup: 7:00 AM - 8:30 AM (depending on route)
  • Evening Return: 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM (depending on route)

How to Apply

Students and staff can apply for the bus facility at the Administrative Office. The following documents are required:

  • Completed application form
  • ID card copy
  • Residential address proof
  • Two passport-sized photographs
